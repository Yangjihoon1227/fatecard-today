# FateCard.today v16 — copyright-safe tarot artwork

This version replaces pure element-style card faces with original tarot-style SVG illustrations for all 78 cards.

## Legal/design decision

- Do not copy modern commercial tarot decks.
- Do not reuse scanned Rider-Waite-Smith recolors from unknown websites.
- Use original symbolic SVG art generated for FateCard.today.
- The images reference tarot archetypes and suit systems, but they are new compositions.

## Future upgrade

For a more premium look, generate 78 high-quality original illustrations with Nano Banana or another image model using the included prompt pack. Keep prompts archetypal and avoid requesting direct copies of Rider-Waite, Thoth, Disney, anime, or any named copyrighted deck/style.
