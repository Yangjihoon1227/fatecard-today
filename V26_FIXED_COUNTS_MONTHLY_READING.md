# FateCard.today v26

## UX fixes

- Stronger active menu contrast so selected menu items no longer look washed out.
- Reading section headings have more space and no icon clipping.
- Custom scrollbars replace the harsh default white bars.
- Menu readings now use fixed card counts:
  - Today: 1 card
  - Love Tarot: 5 cards
  - 3-card Reading: 3 cards
  - Yes/No: 1 card
  - Birth Card: 3 cards
  - 6·12 Month Reading: 18 cards
- Added 6·12 Month Reading as an initially free, later-paid premium feature.
- 6·12 Month Reading includes name, birth date, gender, and job/situation fields.
- Long-term reading creates extended sections for 6 months, 12 months, relationships, work, money, health, opportunity, caution, and action plan.

## Legal/art note

- The card backs are original site artwork.
- The card fronts continue to use the public-domain 1909 RWS file route already used in v23/v24.
- Do not use modern commercial recolors, box art, proprietary card backs, or copied interpretation text.
