# FateCard.today v28

## Added

- After cards are revealed, the card-picking area becomes a wide full-result canvas.
- Selected cards are shown as a full visual summary.
- Every selected card gets its own explanation:
  - card position
  - card name
  - situation meaning
  - card advice
- Integrated reading appears below the per-card explanations.
- AI question/chat area moved to the bottom.
- Enter sends the AI question unless Shift+Enter is used.
- A small send button is added to the bottom-right of the question textarea.
- Submitted question is visibly shown and reflected in the reading after reveal.

## UX logic

Before reveal:
full spread + right waiting panel + bottom AI question box.

After reveal:
full-width result canvas + per-card detail + integrated reading + bottom AI question box.
