## Legal image direction

The design should not copy a named commercial tarot deck. It can use archetypal tarot concepts, vertical card frames, gold borders, celestial symbols, and original compositions. For a premium upgrade, generate original 78-card illustrations with Nano Banana using archetype-based prompts, not deck-copy prompts.
