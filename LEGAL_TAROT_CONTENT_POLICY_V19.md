# FateCard.today v19 — copyright and content policy

## Artwork

This version does not use modern commercial tarot deck scans or images.

The card art is original SVG artwork generated for FateCard.today. It is inspired by traditional tarot symbolism and pre-1910 public-domain occult/card visual language, but it intentionally avoids direct copying of a specific deck, scan, box design, card back, recolored edition, or modern artist's style.

Safe direction:
- Tarot names and archetypes
- Major/minor arcana structure
- Cups, swords, wands, pentacles symbolism
- Celestial, robe, pillar, moon, sun, star, wheel, scale, tower, garden motifs
- Original composition, frame, color, arrangement, and UI treatment

Avoid:
- Modern commercial deck images
- Exact Rider-Waite-Smith scans from random sites
- "Make it exactly like [named deck]"
- Copying card-by-card composition too closely

## Written interpretations

The card meanings are rewritten in original language. They are based on common tarot traditions and general symbolic meaning, not copied from a specific website, book, app, or commercial guidebook.

Tarot meanings as ideas, concepts, and systems are not the same as copying someone else's exact written expression. For safety, this project uses original paraphrased explanations and should continue to avoid copying long passages from existing tarot sites.
